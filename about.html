About BeautiFlow

BeautiFlow is a beauty business management website designed for estheticians, hairstylists, nail technicians, and other beauty professionals. The goal is to make booking, client management, and daily business tasks feel simple and stress-free.

This website was created to show how technology can support beauty entrepreneurs by giving them a clean, organized, and professional online platform.
